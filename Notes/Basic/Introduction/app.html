What are IT Gadgets?
  • IT gadgets are devices used for information and communication, such as smartphones, laptops, TV, tablets, etc.
  • They make communication, learning, work, and entertainment easier and faster.
Important IT Gadgets
Mobile Phone
Calling, messaging, internet, apps, camera.
Laptop
Portable computer for work, study, and entertainment.
Tablet
Touchscreen device for reading, drawing, and online classes.
Television
Broadcasts news, education, and entertainment.
Desktop Computer
Stationary computer for office, school, home.
GPS Device
Navigation and location tracking.
Major Applications
  • Education: E-learning, online exams, digital books.
  • Business: Online meetings, data storage, automation.
  • Healthcare: Patient records, telemedicine, research.
  • Banking: Online transactions, ATMs, security.
  • Home: Entertainment, shopping, communication.
